Our Process

A proven, transparent path from discovery to deployment—and beyond.

How We Work With You

Every engagement follows a structured, collaborative process designed to deliver results quickly and sustainably.

1
Week 1 • 60-90 Minutes

Discovery Meeting

We start with a comprehensive Discovery Meeting to understand your business, challenges, and goals. This is your free AI analysis—no strings attached.

What You Get:
  • Current workflow and bottleneck analysis
  • Automation opportunity identification
  • Projected time and cost savings estimate
  • High-level roadmap and timeline
  • Clear next steps (if you decide to proceed)
2
Week 2 • 3-5 Days

Strategy & Planning

If you decide to move forward, we dive deeper into your systems, workflows, and requirements. We design a detailed automation strategy tailored to your business.

What You Get:
  • Detailed technical roadmap
  • System architecture and integration plan
  • Clear project timeline and milestones
  • Transparent pricing breakdown
  • Success metrics and KPIs definition
3
Weeks 3-6 • Varies by Project

Implementation

This is where the magic happens. Our team builds, configures, and integrates your AI automation systems. We work in sprints with regular check-ins to ensure alignment.

What You Get:
  • Custom-built automation systems
  • Integration with your existing tools
  • Data migration and system configuration
  • Regular progress updates and demos
  • Testing and quality assurance
4
Week 7 • 1 Week

Training & Launch

We don't just build and disappear. We train your entire team on how to use the new systems effectively, ensuring smooth adoption and maximum value.

What You Get:
  • Comprehensive team training sessions
  • Step-by-step documentation and guides
  • Video tutorials for reference
  • Phased rollout to minimize disruption
  • Launch support and troubleshooting
5
Ongoing

Optimization & Support

Our partnership doesn't end at launch. We continuously monitor performance, gather feedback, and refine your systems to ensure they deliver maximum value over time.

What You Get:
  • 30-day post-launch check-ins
  • Performance monitoring and reporting
  • System refinements and improvements
  • Priority support for issues
  • Ongoing consultation and strategy sessions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does implementation typically take?
Most projects are completed within 4-8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Simpler automations can be deployed in as little as 2-3 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions may take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the Strategy & Planning phase.
What if I'm not tech-savvy?
That's exactly why we exist! We handle all the technical complexity and train your team on simple, user-friendly systems. Our clients range from solo business owners to large corporations—no technical expertise required.
Do you work with my existing software?
Yes! We specialize in integrating with your current tools—whether that's your CRM, accounting software, scheduling system, or industry-specific platforms. We build bridges between your existing tools to create seamless workflows.
What happens if something breaks?
All projects include post-launch support. We monitor your systems, provide troubleshooting, and make adjustments as needed. For ongoing peace of mind, we offer optional monthly support packages.
How do I know if AI automation is right for my business?
That's what the free Discovery Meeting is for! We'll analyze your workflow, identify opportunities, and give you honest advice—even if that means AI automation isn't the right fit yet. No pressure, just valuable insights.
Can I start small and scale up?
Absolutely! Many clients start with one automation (like CRM) and expand as they see results. We design systems with scalability in mind, so adding new automations later is seamless.
What industries do you work with?
We specialize in service companies (plumbing, landscaping, roofing, home improvement) and corporate/enterprise clients. That said, we've successfully automated businesses across many industries. If you have repetitive tasks, we can help.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes! We offer flexible payment terms based on project size. Typically, we structure payments in milestones (e.g., 50% at kickoff, 25% at implementation, 25% at launch). Contact us to discuss options.
